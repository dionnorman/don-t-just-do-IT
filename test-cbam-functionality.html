üéØ CBAM Model Overview

The Seven Stages of Concern:

Stage 0 - Unconcerned: Little concern or involvement
Stage 1 - Informational: General awareness and interest
Stage 2 - Personal: Uncertainty about personal impact
Stage 3 - Management: Focus on processes and logistics
Stage 4 - Consequence: Focus on student impact
Stage 5 - Collaboration: Focus on coordinating with others
Stage 6 - Refocusing: Focus on modifications and improvements
